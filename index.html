Our Story

Founded in 2010, Lake Coffee began as a small coffee cart in the heart of Dhaka, Bangladesh. Our passion for exceptional coffee quickly gained a loyal following, allowing us to expand into a full-service coffee shop and online retailer.

Today, we continue our mission to bring the world's finest coffee to enthusiasts everywhere, maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the perfect Coffee.
